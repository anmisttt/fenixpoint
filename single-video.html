The DLC
New challenges await in our latest expansion: Blood and Titanium, available as you read this! Expand the power and versatility of Phoenix Point operatives with cybernetic augmentations. Unlock new abilities for your soldiers, new research projects, and new approaches to tactical combat. But beware: enemies will bring cybernetics to bear against your team, too. Blood and Titanium also introduces a new subfaction of New Jericho, “The Pure”, former soldiers who have replaced most, if not all of their human bodies with bionic upgrades. The Pure come with their own set of missions for you to complete. Discover their story with all-new in-game cinematics. The addition of Bionics adds new possibilities for the player. With the appropriate technologies discovered, your soldiers can be enhanced with their own bionic upgrades, giving them improved combat stats and powerful abilities. This comes at a cost! Bionics are expensive to create, and if damaged, need to be repaired. How much of your humanity are you willing to sacrifice to save the Earth? Blood and Titanium also includes new weapons and equipment, such as the Zeus EMP Grenade - a great way to shut down those fast Pure soldiers and equally effective against vehicles.
