DERLETH UPDATE - V1.0.57335
Today we've released update 1.0.57335, "Derleth," which addresses a number of bugs, balance changes, and makes some key gameplay updates. This includes a reduction to acid damage. Since we know acid is a major issue for many players, we have plans to further address the way which acid works in a future patch.
