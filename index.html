Know your
Enemy
Explore wiki
Scylla
Massive, commanding units that control Pandoran colonies and are capable of leveling entire buildings as they search for prey.
Chiron
Armored artillery units, capable of launching devastating mortar strikes or autonomous Pandoran worms throughout the entire battlefield.
Siren
Elite units that use telepathic nightmares to take control of enemy soldiers and are capable of devastating close-range attacks.
Arthron
Specializing in melee and short-range combat, Arthrons are the backbone of the Pandoran forces and focus on overwhelming numbers and sheer strength to achieve victory.
