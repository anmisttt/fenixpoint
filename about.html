About Snapshot Games
Snapshot Games is a video game developer and publisher based in Sofia, Bulgaria. Founded by Julian Gollop and David Kaye, Snapshot's first game was the crowdfunded and critically praised Chaos Reborn.

Snapshot's latest title, Phoenix Point, is a sci-fi strategy and tactical combat game which puts players in control of humanity’s last remnants against an apocalyptic alien threat
